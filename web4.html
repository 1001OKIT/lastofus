WEB4

Web4 is a future concept of the internet that goes beyond Web3 by adding advanced artificial intelligence and human-machine interaction. It’s sometimes called the “symbiotic web” because humans and machines work closely together.

How it would work

  • Powered by advanced AI systems (like ChatGPT, but much more powerful)
  • Devices, apps, and systems constantly connected and learning
  • The internet predicts your needs and makes decisions for you

Examples of Web4 features

  • Personalized AI assistants that anticipate your needs
  • Smart environments that adapt to your preferences
  • Seamless integration of virtual and augmented reality
