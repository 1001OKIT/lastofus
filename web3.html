WEB3

Web3 is the next stage of the internet focused on decentralization and user ownership. Instead of big companies controlling your data (like social media platforms today), Web3 uses blockchain technology so users control their own information and digital assets.

How it works

  • Built on blockchain networks like Ethereum.
  • Uses cryptocurrencies and digital wallets (e.g., MetaMask)
  • Runs decentralized apps (dApps) without central control

Examples

  • Decentralized finance (DeFi) platforms like Uniswap
  • Non-fungible tokens (NFTs) for digital art and collectibles
  • Decentralized social media platforms like Mastodon
