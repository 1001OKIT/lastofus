Multiplier

A multiplier is a device or circuit that performs multiplication of two numbers. In digital electronics, multipliers are used in various applications such as signal processing, computer arithmetic, and digital filters.

It is important to note that a multiplier is a fundamental component in many digital systems, enabling efficient mathematical operations and data processing.
